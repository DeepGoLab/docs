How to Check Ratings

Rating#

You can:

  1. sort tokens according to Value/APY/Ratings on the Rating page,

  2. click the token name to enter the details page,

  3. see the following information of the project within 180 days on the details page:

    • Market Trend
    • Key Info

  4. see the visual display of the token rating on the details page,

    • Winning
    • Revenue
    • Efficiency
    • Stability
    • Drawdown
    • Sustainability

    the above are the main indicators.

My Favorites#

You can:

  1. select your favorite tokens on the Rating page and light up the star to collect,
  2. view and manage your Favorite Tokens in the Space page.
